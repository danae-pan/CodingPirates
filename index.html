### Color Detection #### Human Vision When we see something, light from the object enters our eyes through a clear layer called the cornea. The cornea directs the light to the pupils, which control how much light gets in. The light then reaches the retina at the back of the eye, where special sensors called rods and cones detect colors and shapes.
